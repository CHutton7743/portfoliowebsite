Our Goal

Consolidate

We have noticed that during our onboarding for the internship, employees have to go... Well, everywhere to get things done. We want to consolidate that to a one-stop shop website to act as a central hub for employees to finish all of their onboarding process.

Streamline

The central hub site should have a list of all teams at Verint, so that employees can easily find the team they belong to. Once found there should be a step-by-step process on the team page guiding employees on how to get their onboarding tasks done efficiently. Thereby automating the process without involving team management.

Aid ramp up time

We noticed that when we joined the company there was a solid 2 weeks where we just could not get anything done because of onboarding tasks or environment isnt set up etc etc. Our goal with this is to automate and streamline the process as much as possible to get new employees ready to work quicker.
